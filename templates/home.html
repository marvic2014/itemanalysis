Welcome to the ZipGrade Item Analysis Web Tool

This tool helps you generate an item analysis report from your ZipGrade Standard Excel export.

Instructions:

1. Login your zipgrade account at zipgrade.com.

2. Click the "Quiz" tab.

3. Select and click the Quiz name you wanted to generate a report.

4. Scroll down, under "Quiz Statistics" click the "Excel" dropdown.

5. Click the "Standard Format-XLSX".

6. Select student percentage you like to consider.

7. Choose the file that you downloaded from zipgrade.

8. Click "Start Analysis".
